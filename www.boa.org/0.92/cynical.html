Cynical about Boa?

The cynical among you may have checked the headers, and found out that www.boa.org is served by Apache 1.1.3. This is no reason for cynicism: Russ Nelson had the ns.crynwr.com machine set up long before he heard of Boa, and has graciously donated space on it to give Boa a real 'net presence. He couldn't possibly switch to Boa until it has complete Virtual host support, which is still lacking in release 0.92. Even then (smart man) he makes no promises.

The maintainers do operate Boa servers on the web, and in fact mirror this web site there. Check out

  • recycle.cebaf.gov
  • x183-191.reshalls.umn.edu
where you can download Boa from a working copy of Boa!
